About

The Artist

Born and raised in Chicago, Illinois, Christina Workman has been working with oil paints since the tender age of three.

After studying Ceramics, Fine Art, and Photography at Columbia College and The Art Institute of Chicago, Christina relocated to Los Angeles, California, where she currently resides.

Her work spans bold abstract expressionism, serene landscapes, hand-built ceramics, and handmade healing crystal jewelry. When she's not creating, Christina spends her free time at the beach with her son and two dogs.
